# Shipping & Delivery

## Delivery timelines
- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days (free on orders over ₹999 / $25).
- **Express shipping:** 1–2 business days (flat fee shown at checkout).
- Orders placed after 2 PM local time ship the next business day.

## Tracking an order
Every shipped order gets a tracking link by email. If a customer can't find it, ask
for their **order number** and confirm the email on file, then resend the tracking
link. Tracking can take up to 24 hours to show movement after a label is created.

## Delayed or stuck shipments
If tracking has not updated in **5+ business days**, treat the order as delayed:
apologize, offer to either reship or refund, and flag the carrier. Do not promise a
specific new delivery date you cannot guarantee.

## Lost packages
If a carrier marks a package delivered but the customer did not receive it, ask them
to check with neighbors/building reception and wait 48 hours; if still missing,
reship at no cost.

## International shipping
International orders may incur customs duties paid by the recipient. Delivery is
7–21 business days depending on destination and customs clearance.
