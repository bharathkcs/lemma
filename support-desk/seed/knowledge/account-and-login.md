# Account & Login Help

## Password reset
Direct the customer to the **"Forgot password"** link on the sign-in page. The reset
email arrives within a few minutes; tell them to check spam. Reset links expire after
**60 minutes** — if expired, they can request a new one.

## Can't access the account email
If the customer no longer has access to the email on file, verify their identity using
the **last order number and billing ZIP/postal code**, then update the email on file.
Never change account email based on an unverified request.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA) issues
If a customer is locked out due to 2FA, confirm identity (last order + billing detail),
then disable 2FA on their account and ask them to re-enroll a fresh device.

## Updating account details
Customers can update name, shipping address, and communication preferences under
**Settings → Profile**. Email and payment methods require identity verification.

## Account deletion / data requests
For account deletion or a copy of personal data, confirm identity and route the
request to the privacy team; acknowledge within 72 hours.
