# Refund & Returns Policy

## Eligibility window
Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of the delivery date. After
30 days, refunds are issued as **store credit** only. Items must be unused and in
original packaging unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

## How refunds are processed
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- Processing time is **5–7 business days** after the return is received and inspected.
- The customer receives an automated email confirmation once the refund is initiated.

## Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged or defective, the customer is entitled to a **full refund
or free replacement** with no return shipping cost, regardless of the 30-day window.
Ask the customer to share a photo of the damage to speed up processing.

## Non-refundable items
Gift cards, downloadable software, and final-sale items marked "clearance" are not
refundable.

## Duplicate / double charges
If a customer reports being charged twice for one order, confirm the order number,
verify the duplicate in billing, and issue an immediate refund of the duplicate
charge. Apologize and confirm the 5–7 business day window for the reversal to appear.
